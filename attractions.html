Gunung Lang Ipoh

Gunung Lang, located just a few kilometers from Ipoh's city center, is a stunning recreational park and a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a peaceful escape. Nestled amidst limestone hills, this park is centered around a pristine lake and offers breathtaking views of rugged karst formations, lush greenery, and serene waters. The main highlight of Gunung Lang is its picturesque boat ride across the lake, which transports visitors to a beautifully landscaped area featuring gardens, pavilions, and a mini zoo. The park's walking trails, observation towers, and picnic spots make it an ideal destination for families, couples, and anyone looking to unwind in a tranquil natural setting.

Tasik Cermin Ipoh

Tasik Cermin, or the "Mirror Lake," is a hidden gem in Ipoh, offering a serene and breathtaking natural retreat. Tucked away amidst towering limestone cliffs, this secluded lake gets its name from its crystal-clear, glass-like surface that reflects the surrounding rock formations and greenery, creating a mesmerizing mirror effect. Located near Gunung Rapat, Tasik Cermin is accessible through a tunnel carved into the limestone, which adds a sense of adventure to the journey. Once there, visitors are greeted by a tranquil oasis, perfect for photography, relaxation, or simply soaking in the natural beauty.
